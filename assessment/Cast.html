EDWARD THOMAS HARDY

Born:

15 September 1977

Occupation:

Actor

Years active:

2001–present

Edward Thomas "Tom" Hardy is an English movie, television, stage, voice actor and screenwriter. He is known for his roles in Star Trek: Nemesis, RocknRolla, Bronson, Inception, Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy, This Means War, and the television series Stuart: A Life Backwards. For his work in that series, he earned a nomination for a BAFTA Award for Best Actor. Hardy also played Max Rockatansky in Mad Max: Fury Road and as Venom in the 2018 movie Venom. Hardy appeared as Farrier in the 2017 Nolan movie Dunkirk. He is an only child. His parents were Anne and Edward "Chips" Hardy. He was raised in East Sheen, London. Hardy married Sarah Ward in 1999. They divorced in 2004. He has a son with ex-girlfriend Rachael Speed, Louis Thomas Hardy. Hardy proposed to actress Charlotte Riley. They were married in 2014.

MICHELLE INGRID WILLIAMS

Born:

September 9, 1980

Occupation:

Actor

Years active:

1993–present

Michelle Ingrid Williams is an American actress. Daughter of the politician and trader Larry R. Williams, she was raised in Kalispell and San Diego. She began her career with television guest appearances and made her film debut in the family film Lassie in 1994. This was followed by low-profile films, before having her breakthrough with the drama film Brokeback Mountain. Her highest-grossing releases came with the thriller Shutter Island (2010), the fantasy film Oz the Great and Powerful (2013), the musical The Greatest Showman (2017), and the superhero film Venom (2018). Williams is an advocate for equal pay. She is reticent about her personal life; she has a daughter from her relationship with the late actor Heath Ledger, and was briefly married to the musician Phil Elverum. She is married to theater director Thomas Kail, with whom she has a child.
